Rincón Limeño

I enjoy this restaurant because it has authentic Peruvian food. I love eating "arroz chaufa" which is Peruvian-Chinese fusion style fried rice. I also enjoy the different drinks they have such as Pisco Sour, one of Peru's most famous cocktails. The staff are friendly and there is always space, never any long waits. Great place to try some new latin foods!
